# Main Companions

## Overview

The companions are not simply party members.

Each companion represents a competing vision for the future of Aetheria.

Each challenges the protagonist in different ways.

Together they form the emotional and philosophical core of the story.

---

# Kael Astra

## Title

The Dragon Heir

## Represents

Reality

## Background

The last surviving heir of an ancient Dragon King lineage.

Raised to become a guardian of the world.

## Core Belief

Order must be preserved.

## Internal Conflict

Duty versus freedom.

## Relationship With Player

One of the first companions.

Initially distrusts the protagonist because of Aetherion's legacy.

---

# Sera Nhal

## Title

The Naga Archivist

## Represents

Memory

## Background

Keeper of a forbidden Memory Archive.

Possesses knowledge that could change history.

## Core Belief

Truth must never be hidden.

## Internal Conflict

Truth versus compassion.

## Relationship With Player

Helps uncover lost memories.

Questions whether the world is ready for the truth.

---

# Liora Vale

## Title

The Spirit Wanderer

## Represents

Soul

## Background

A traveler capable of crossing boundaries between the mortal world and the Spirit Realm.

## Core Belief

Every soul deserves its own path.

## Internal Conflict

Acceptance versus attachment.

## Relationship With Player

Understands the burden of reincarnation better than anyone else.

---

# Orion Lux

## Title

The Celestial Remnant

## Represents

Heaven

## Background

A surviving intelligence from the Age of Heaven.

Neither fully human nor fully machine.

## Core Belief

The Celestial Age can be restored.

## Internal Conflict

Progress versus wisdom.

## Relationship With Player

Possesses knowledge about Aetherion that no one else remembers.

---

# Rowan Ashford

## Title

The Human Champion

## Represents

Choice

## Background

A renowned adventurer from the Kingdoms of Men.

Unaffiliated with dragons, nagas, spirits, or Celestial systems.

## Core Belief

Humanity should determine its own future.

## Internal Conflict

Independence versus responsibility.

## Relationship With Player

Acts as the grounding voice of ordinary people.

---

# Party Philosophy

Each companion represents a different answer to the world's central question.

What should become of Heaven's legacy?

No companion is entirely correct.

No companion is entirely wrong.

The player must decide whose beliefs they agree with.

---

# Endgame Role

By the final act, every companion develops a personal stance regarding:

- The Heaven Engine
- The Silence
- Aetherion
- The future of the world

The player's decisions influence these outcomes.

Some companions may become lifelong allies.

Some may leave.

Some may become opponents.

---

# Narrative Theme

The companions are reflections of the choices available to the protagonist.

They are not followers.

They are people with their own convictions, hopes, fears, and futures.
