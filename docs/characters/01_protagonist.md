# The Protagonist

## Overview

The protagonist is the player's avatar within the world of Aetheria.

Unlike a traditional predefined hero, the protagonist is customizable.

However, they are not a blank slate.

Every version of the protagonist shares the same soul.

The soul of Emperor Aetherion.

The final ruler of the Celestial Age.

---

## Narrative Role

The protagonist serves as the bridge between:

- Past and present
- Myth and truth
- Humanity and Heaven
- Memory and reality
- Destiny and choice

The entire story is experienced through their journey.

---

## Character Philosophy

The player may choose who the protagonist becomes.

The player may not choose what the protagonist is.

The protagonist is always:

- Reincarnated
- Connected to Heaven
- Tied to the Fall
- Central to the fate of the world

The mystery is not who the protagonist was.

The mystery is whether they will repeat the mistakes of the past.

---

# Customization Framework

## Appearance

Players may customize:

- Gender presentation
- Face
- Hair
- Skin tone
- Body appearance
- Voice

Customization exists to support player identity.

It does not alter the narrative.

---

## Background Selection

Players choose an origin background.

This influences dialogue, flavor, and early-game advantages.

### Village Hunter

Knowledge of forests and survival.

### Shrine Keeper

Connection to spiritual traditions.

### Merchant Family

Social awareness and negotiation.

### Orphan

Independent outlook and resilience.

### Soldier's Child

Combat familiarity and discipline.

These backgrounds shape perspective.

They do not change destiny.

---

# Core Personality Arc

At the beginning of the game, the protagonist believes they are ordinary.

The discovery of ancient memories gradually challenges that belief.

The central character arc explores identity.

Key question:

> Are we bound by our past, or defined by our choices?

---

# Relationship to Aetherion

The protagonist is not merely carrying Aetherion's memories.

The protagonist is the continuation of the same soul.

However, they are also a different person.

Different experiences create different choices.

Throughout the story, players must decide how much of Aetherion they are willing to embrace.

---

# Early Memories

The first memories that awaken are fragmented.

Burning skies.

Falling cities.

Dying dragons.

A shattered celestial ring.

A voice asking:

"Will you make the same choice again?"

The meaning of these memories remains unclear for much of the game.

---

# The Four Paths

The protagonist naturally develops affinity toward four themes.

## Reality

Associated with Dragons.

Focus:

- Combat mastery
- Physical strength
- Protection

## Memory

Associated with Nagas.

Focus:

- Knowledge
- Investigation
- Ancient languages

## Soul

Associated with Spirits.

Focus:

- Spiritual insight
- Empathy
- Reincarnation abilities

## Heaven

Associated with Celestial technology.

Focus:

- Relics
- Timeline abilities
- Lost systems

Players may specialize or combine paths.

---

# Relationship With the World

Different groups react to the protagonist differently.

Dragons recognize familiar traces within their soul.

Nagas sense connections to forgotten history.

Spirits perceive multiple timelines surrounding them.

Ancient Celestial systems identify them as a known individual.

Many characters recognize the protagonist before the protagonist understands why.

---

# Internal Conflict

The protagonist struggles with three fears.

### Fear of Destiny

What if history cannot be changed?

### Fear of Truth

What if Aetherion truly caused the Fall?

### Fear of Identity

What if they are becoming someone else?

These fears drive much of the emotional narrative.

---

# Endgame Transformation

By the final act, the protagonist understands the truth of Heaven.

The player must decide what kind of person they will become.

Possible outcomes include:

- Restorer of the original timeline
- Defender of the current world
- Successor to the Heaven Engine
- Creator of a new future

The ending is determined by choices, beliefs, and relationships developed throughout the journey.

---

# Character Theme

The protagonist embodies the central theme of the game.

History shapes us.

Memory guides us.

But ultimately,

The future belongs to those who choose it.
