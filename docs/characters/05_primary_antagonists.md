# Primary Antagonists

## Overview

The story does not revolve around a single villain.

Different antagonists represent different dangers, ideologies, and responses to the legacy of Heaven.

Many are convinced they are saving the world.

---

# High Oracle Veyr

## Title

Leader of the Void Cult

## Role

Primary ideological antagonist.

## Belief

The current world is a false reality created by endless timeline manipulation.

## Goal

Destroy the systems maintaining the fractured timeline.

Allow existence to reset.

## View of Aetherion

The First Sinner.

---

# Dragon King Tharos

## Title

The Sleeping Mountain

## Role

Dragon antagonist.

## Belief

Mortals should never again wield the powers of Heaven.

## Goal

Seal all Celestial knowledge permanently.

## View of Player

A dangerous reminder of the past.

---

# Archivist Sereth

## Title

Keeper of Forgotten Truths

## Role

Naga antagonist.

## Belief

The truth must be preserved regardless of consequences.

## Goal

Reveal all hidden history.

Even if civilizations collapse.

## View of Player

The key to unlocking lost knowledge.

---

# The Ascendant

## Title

The Last Celestial

## Role

Celestial extremist.

## Belief

The Fall was caused by human weakness rather than technology.

## Goal

Restore Heaven exactly as it once existed.

## View of Aetherion

A failed ruler.

---

# The Silence

## Nature

The ultimate mystery.

Not clearly alive.

Not clearly evil.

Not clearly sentient.

## Role

The deepest threat behind the story.

## Belief

Unknown.

## Goal

Unknown.

## Narrative Purpose

Represents the end of memory, possibility, and history.

---

# Antagonist Philosophy

Every major antagonist should force the player to confront an uncomfortable possibility.

What if they are right?

The strongest conflicts arise when opposing viewpoints contain elements of truth.

---

# Narrative Theme

The antagonists are not obstacles.

They are alternative answers to the central questions of the game.

The player must decide which future deserves to survive.
