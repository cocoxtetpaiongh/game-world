# Gameplay Vision

## Purpose

This document defines the player experience of Age of Legends: Echoes of Heaven.

The goal is not simply to create an open-world RPG.

The goal is to create a world that rewards curiosity, discovery, exploration, and understanding.

Players should feel like explorers uncovering truths hidden beneath mythology.

---

# Core Experience

The player is a reincarnated soul carrying the legacy of Emperor Aetherion.

Through exploration, relationships, combat, and discovery, they uncover the truth behind Heaven, the Celestials, and reality itself.

The game is built around wonder.

The player should constantly ask:

"What is this place?"

"What happened here?"

"What does this mean?"

---

# Gameplay Pillars

## Wonder

Wonder is the highest priority.

Every major location should create curiosity.

Every region should contain mysteries.

The world should feel larger than the player's understanding.

---

## Discovery

Knowledge is progression.

Players gain power not only through combat but through understanding.

Discoveries include:

- Memories
- Lost histories
- Celestial records
- Timeline fragments
- Ancient truths

---

## Exploration

Exploration drives the game.

Players are encouraged to:

- Investigate ruins
- Follow legends
- Enter spirit realms
- Explore forgotten regions
- Discover hidden archives

The world should reward curiosity.

---

## Choice

The player's decisions shape relationships, factions, and the future of the world.

Choices should reflect philosophy rather than simple morality.

---

# Open World Philosophy

The world should not feel like a checklist.

Avoid:

- Excessive map icons
- Repetitive activities
- Artificial content inflation

Every major location should have narrative purpose.

Every discovery should matter.

---

# The Ancient Future World

The world appears mythological.

However, it contains remnants of civilizations far beyond modern human understanding.

Players gradually discover:

- Living architecture
- Memory archives
- Celestial gateways
- Reality-engineered structures

These discoveries should feel mysterious rather than technological.

---

# Combat Philosophy

Combat is important.

Combat is not the primary identity of the game.

The goal is meaningful encounters rather than constant fighting.

Every major enemy should tell part of the world's story.

---

# The Four Paths

Players naturally develop affinity toward four major paths.

## Reality

Dragon-aligned path.

Focus:

- Strength
- Defense
- Direct combat

---

## Memory

Naga-aligned path.

Focus:

- Investigation
- Knowledge
- Ancient understanding

---

## Soul

Spirit-aligned path.

Focus:

- Spiritual abilities
- Empathy
- Reincarnation powers

---

## Heaven

Celestial-aligned path.

Focus:

- Relics
- Reality engineering
- Timeline interaction

Players may specialize or combine paths.

---

# Companions

Companions are philosophical counterparts rather than followers.

Each companion represents a different vision for the future.

Their beliefs influence quests, outcomes, and endings.

---

# Timeline Echoes

Timeline Echoes are one of the game's signature features.

Players occasionally witness:

- The Age of Heaven
- Alternate histories
- Lost futures
- Fragments resembling modern timelines

These experiences are brief and meaningful.

The game is not built around traditional time travel.

---

# Progression Philosophy

Power comes from three sources.

- Personal growth
- Relationships
- Understanding

A player who understands the world should feel stronger than a player who only gains equipment.

---

# Emotional Journey

The player journey follows four stages.

Wonder.

Discovery.

Understanding.

Responsibility.

By the end of the story, the player is no longer searching for answers.

They are deciding what future deserves to exist.

---

# Vision Statement

Age of Legends: Echoes of Heaven is an open-world narrative RPG where players explore a mythological world built upon forgotten reality engineering, uncover ancient truths hidden beneath legend, and ultimately decide the future of a universe larger than any civilization can fully understand.
