# Progression Systems

## Purpose

This document defines how players grow stronger throughout Age of Legends: Echoes of Heaven.

Progression should reflect the themes of the world.

The player does not become powerful simply by defeating enemies.

The player becomes powerful through understanding, relationships, experience, and awakening.

---

# Progression Philosophy

Most RPGs focus on:

- Levels
- Statistics
- Equipment

Echoes of Heaven focuses on:

- Knowledge
- Identity
- Mastery
- Relationships
- Awakening

The player should feel like they are evolving rather than grinding.

---

# Five Sources of Progression

## Personal Growth

Traditional character progression.

Includes:

- Health
- Stamina
- Combat mastery
- New abilities

---

## Knowledge Growth

The discovery of truths unlocks new opportunities.

Examples:

- Ancient languages
- Celestial symbols
- Memory protocols
- Dragon rites
- Spirit rituals

Knowledge opens paths unavailable through combat.

---

## Relationship Growth

The player develops bonds with companions, factions, and important characters.

These relationships unlock:

- New quests
- New dialogue
- Companion abilities
- Alternate outcomes

---

## Awakening Growth

The protagonist gradually awakens deeper memories connected to Aetherion.

Awakenings provide:

- New powers
- New knowledge
- New choices
- New responsibilities

---

## World Growth

As the player progresses, regions of the world change.

Cities evolve.

Factions react.

Ruins awaken.

Progression should affect the world itself.

---

# Character Level

The game contains levels.

However, levels should not dominate progression.

A high-level player should not automatically understand ancient mysteries.

A knowledgeable player may access opportunities unavailable to stronger characters.

---

# The Four Paths

The player develops affinity toward four major paths.

---

## Reality Path

Associated with Dragons.

Focus:

- Physical power
- Defense
- Stability
- Direct confrontation

Example abilities:

- Dragon Strength
- Reality Anchor
- Storm Guard

---

## Memory Path

Associated with Nagas.

Focus:

- Investigation
- Ancient knowledge
- Information control
- Historical insight

Example abilities:

- Memory Sight
- Archive Recall
- Forgotten Language

---

## Soul Path

Associated with Spirits.

Focus:

- Empathy
- Spirit interaction
- Reincarnation abilities
- Emotional influence

Example abilities:

- Soul Vision
- Spirit Walk
- Echo Communion

---

## Heaven Path

Associated with Celestial knowledge.

Focus:

- Relics
- Reality engineering
- Timeline interaction
- Celestial systems

Example abilities:

- Glyph Activation
- Echo Stabilization
- Celestial Interface

---

# Knowledge Progression

Knowledge is treated as a progression resource.

Important discoveries are recorded within the player's Codex.

Knowledge unlocks:

- New dialogue
- New investigations
- New region access
- New interpretations of events

The player should feel rewarded for curiosity.

---

# Memory Fragments

Memory Fragments are one of the most important progression systems.

Fragments reveal:

- Aetherion's life
- The Age of Heaven
- The Fall
- The Woman at the End of the Bridge
- Hidden truths about reality

Collecting fragments expands both narrative understanding and gameplay opportunities.

---

# Companion Growth

Companions possess their own progression.

Their development depends upon:

- Shared experiences
- Decisions
- Quest outcomes
- Philosophical alignment

Companions may change dramatically over the course of the story.

---

# Reputation Systems

Major groups track the player's actions.

Examples:

- Dragon Clans
- Naga Archives
- Spirit Courts
- Human Kingdoms
- Celestial Remnants

Reputation influences opportunities rather than serving as a simple morality meter.

---

# Equipment Philosophy

Avoid excessive loot.

Weapons and relics should be meaningful.

A single weapon may remain relevant for much of the game.

Every important item should have:

- History
- Narrative significance
- Unique mechanics

---

# Awakening Milestones

Major story revelations trigger Awakening Events.

These moments represent significant growth of the protagonist.

Examples:

- First Celestial Memory
- Dragon Recognition
- Naga Archive Access
- Spirit Realm Crossing
- Heaven Engine Contact

Each awakening expands both narrative and gameplay possibilities.

---

# Endgame Progression

By the final act, the player should not feel powerful because of numbers.

The player should feel powerful because they understand truths hidden from almost everyone else.

The greatest progression reward is perspective.

---

# Design Rules

## Rule 1

Knowledge must be as valuable as combat power.

## Rule 2

Progression should unlock possibilities, not only increase statistics.

## Rule 3

Every major revelation should provide gameplay value.

## Rule 4

Relationships are progression.

## Rule 5

The player should gradually feel less like an adventurer and more like someone uncovering the structure of reality itself.

---

# Progression Statement

The progression system of Echoes of Heaven is designed to reward curiosity, understanding, and meaningful choices.

The player's greatest power comes not from equipment or levels, but from learning truths hidden beneath myth, history, and reality.
