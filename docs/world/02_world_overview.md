# The World of Aetheria

## Overview

Aetheria is a world built upon forgotten history.

To most people, it is a land of dragons, spirits, ancient temples, celestial relics, and kingdoms struggling to survive in the shadow of legends.

Every culture tells stories about the Age of Heaven.

Every civilization preserves myths about gods who once walked among mortals.

Every ruin is treated as sacred.

Every relic is believed to contain divine power.

Most people accept these stories as truth.

They are wrong.

---

## The Hidden Truth

The beings remembered as gods were not gods.

They were the Celestials.

An advanced civilization that existed thousands of years before recorded history.

Their mastery of science, energy, gravity, memory, and time became mythology after their fall.

Their cities became ruins.

Their machines became relics.

Their guardians became dragons.

Their experiments became monsters.

Their history became religion.

The modern world lives among the remains of a civilization it no longer understands.

---

## The Five Ages

### The Age of Origins

The earliest age.

Little is known.

Ancient spirits, primordial dragons, and the first intelligent civilizations emerged during this era.

### The Age of Dragons

The world belonged to dragons.

Great dragon kingdoms ruled the skies, mountains, oceans, and rivers.

### The Age of Heaven

The rise of the Celestials.

Technology advanced beyond anything the world had ever seen.

Floating cities crossed the skies.

The Heaven Engine was constructed.

### The Fall of Heaven

The Heaven Engine began altering history.

Reality fractured.

Timelines collided.

The Celestial Empire collapsed.

### The Age of Legends

The current era.

Thousands of years after the Fall.

Most knowledge of the Celestials has been lost.

Myth has replaced history.

---

## The State of the World

The world is not at peace.

Old rivalries continue.

Ancient powers stir.

Dragons awaken.

Forgotten ruins reactivate.

Strange celestial phenomena appear more frequently every year.

Something is changing.

Something is returning.

---

## What Ordinary People Believe

- The gods abandoned the world.
- Dragons are divine beings.
- Spirits protect sacred places.
- Celestial relics contain holy power.
- The Age of Heaven was an age of miracles.
- Reincarnation is guided by fate.

---

## What Is Actually True

The Celestials never vanished completely.

Their machines still operate.

Fragments of their consciousness still exist.

The Heaven Engine still survives.

History is still being altered.

Almost nobody realizes it.

---

## The Great Mystery

Why are ancient ruins awakening?

Why are dragons becoming active again?

Why are forgotten memories returning to certain individuals?

Why do different versions of history appear across the world?

Why does the name Aetherion appear repeatedly throughout ancient records?

The answers form the central mystery of Aetheria.

---

## Narrative Theme

> History is not forgotten.
>
> History is buried.
>
> And buried things eventually awaken.
