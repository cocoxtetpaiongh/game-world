# Cosmology Bible

## Purpose

This document defines the deepest truths of the setting.

Most of this knowledge is never directly revealed to players.

Its purpose is to keep the world internally consistent.

---

# The Great Principle

The universe is larger than any civilization.

Larger than humanity.

Larger than the Celestials.

Larger than Dragons.

Larger than Nagas.

No culture possesses the complete truth.

Every civilization understands only part of reality.

---

# The Three Pillars

Reality is built upon three fundamental pillars.

## Reality

The structure of existence.

Associated with Dragons.

Represents:

- Matter
- Space
- Stability
- Possibility becoming real

---

## Memory

The preservation of existence.

Associated with Nagas.

Represents:

- History
- Knowledge
- Identity
- Continuity

---

## Soul

The essence that survives change.

Associated with Spirits.

Represents:

- Consciousness
- Reincarnation
- Meaning
- Growth

---

# The Ancient Balance

Before the rise of humanity and before the Age of Heaven, Reality, Memory, and Soul existed in equilibrium.

Dragons, Nagas, and Spirits each protected different aspects of this balance.

The world evolved naturally.

---

# Humanity

Humanity is unique.

Unlike Dragons, Nagas, or Spirits, humans possess unusual adaptability.

They are capable of learning from all three pillars.

This flexibility eventually allowed the rise of the Celestial Civilization.

---

# The Celestials

The Celestials were not gods.

They were explorers.

They became the most advanced civilization known to history.

They mastered:

- Memory engineering
- Consciousness architecture
- Spatial manipulation
- Timeline observation
- Reality modeling

Yet they remained limited.

The soul continued to elude them.

---

# Reality Engineering

The Celestials did not view reality as fixed.

They viewed reality as a system that could be studied, modeled, and influenced.

Many phenomena later called magic were applications of Reality Engineering.

Examples include:

- Celestial Gates
- Memory Archives
- Living Cities
- Timeline Anchors

To later civilizations these appeared supernatural.

---

# The Heaven Engine

The Heaven Engine was not a machine.

It was a civilization-scale instrument.

Its purpose was to understand and protect existence.

It could:

- Observe timelines
- Model futures
- Influence probability
- Preserve historical continuity

Over time its use expanded beyond its original purpose.

This contributed to the Fall of Heaven.

---

# The Great Discovery

The Heaven Engine eventually uncovered evidence that reality itself was changing.

Not civilization.

Not politics.

Reality.

Something deeper than any known natural process was affecting existence.

This discovery terrified the Celestials.

---

# The Silence

The Silence is not a monster.

It is not an army.

It is not a god.

The Silence is the gradual death of possibility.

A state where:

- No new ideas emerge
- No meaningful change occurs
- No new futures form
- No souls continue their journey

Reality becomes perfectly stable.

And completely lifeless.

---

# Why Aetherion Acted

Aetherion did not attempt to control history because he desired power.

He believed reality itself was approaching collapse.

The Heaven Engine appeared to offer a solution.

The tragedy is that he may have accelerated the very process he hoped to stop.

---

# The Mystery Before Heaven

The oldest Celestial records describe impossible discoveries.

Structures older than recorded history.

Patterns embedded within reality itself.

Phenomena that existed before the earliest known civilizations.

The Celestials never fully understood these findings.

---

# Dragons and Nagas

Neither species was created by the Celestials.

Both predate the Age of Heaven.

Many scholars believe Dragons and Nagas evolved alongside deeper laws of existence.

Their origins remain uncertain.

---

# Spirits and Souls

The greatest limitation of Celestial knowledge concerned the soul.

They could preserve memories.

They could preserve minds.

They could preserve information.

But they could never fully reproduce a living soul.

This mystery remains unresolved.

---

# Present and Future Echoes

Timeline fractures occasionally reveal alternate eras.

Some resemble the modern world.

Others resemble advanced futures.

These are not intended as primary settings.

They exist only as fragments of possibility.

The player encounters them as echoes rather than destinations.

---

# Final Truth

The universe remains larger than any explanation.

Mythology is not false.

Science is not false.

Each is a language used to describe the same reality.

The Celestials understood more than humanity.

The Dragons understood things the Celestials did not.

The Spirits understand things that remain hidden from all others.

And beyond all of them lies a truth that no civilization has fully discovered.

---

# Creator Rule

Never fully explain the universe.

Wonder is a core feature of the setting.

Every answer should reveal a deeper question.

The world must always feel larger than the knowledge of its inhabitants.
