# Timeline Bible

## Purpose

The Timeline Bible defines the rules of time, memory, reincarnation, and historical alteration within Aetheria.

These rules exist to keep the world internally consistent.

Time travel is not magic.

It is a consequence of damage caused by the Heaven Engine during the Fall of Heaven.

---

## The Nature of Time

Most people believe time is a river.

The Celestials discovered that time is closer to a living network.

Every decision creates possibilities.

Most possibilities naturally collapse.

Only one dominant timeline normally survives.

Before the Fall of Heaven, reality remained stable because these possibilities continuously resolved themselves.

The Heaven Engine changed that.

---

## The First Fracture

When the Heaven Engine altered history for the first time, reality accepted the correction.

When it altered history a second time, reality resisted.

After thousands of interventions, the network of possible histories became unstable.

Instead of disappearing, abandoned timelines remained connected to reality.

These abandoned timelines became known as Fractures.

---

## Timeline Fractures

A Fracture is a historical possibility that should no longer exist.

Examples:

- A kingdom that was erased from history.
- A war that never happened.
- A ruler who died in one timeline but survived in another.
- A city destroyed in one age but thriving in another.

Most fractures remain hidden.

Some become physical locations where multiple histories overlap.

These locations are among the most dangerous places in the world.

---

## Echo Zones

Echo Zones are areas where different versions of history bleed together.

Within an Echo Zone:

- People may witness events from the past.
- Buildings may appear and disappear.
- Dead individuals may briefly exist.
- Different historical outcomes may coexist.

Many spirit forests and ancient ruins contain small Echo Zones.

The largest Echo Zones are connected to major Celestial sites.

---

## Memory and Reality

The greatest consequence of timeline damage is memory instability.

Reality remembers every version of itself.

Most living beings cannot access those memories.

Some individuals can.

These individuals are called Echo-Born.

The player is one of them.

---

## Reincarnation

Reincarnation exists naturally within the world.

However, the player's reincarnation is not entirely natural.

Before the Fall, fragments of Emperor Aetherion's consciousness became embedded within a Celestial preservation system.

Across thousands of years, those fragments repeatedly resurfaced through different lives.

Each incarnation carries pieces of memories from previous timelines.

Most never awaken.

The player's incarnation does.

---

## Why the Player Remembers

The player can access memories because they exist at the intersection of multiple timelines.

They do not remember a single past.

They remember many pasts.

This is why some memories contradict each other.

In one timeline Aetherion may have been a hero.

In another, a tyrant.

In another, a sacrifice.

The player's challenge is discovering which truths matter.

---

## Time Travel

True time travel is extremely rare.

The player does not move freely through history.

Instead, they access Anchors.

Anchors are moments of extreme historical significance.

Examples:

- The creation of the Heaven Engine.
- The beginning of the Fall.
- The death of a dragon king.
- The collapse of a floating city.

By entering Anchors, the player can witness or influence specific events.

---

## The Cost of Alteration

Every historical change has consequences.

The greater the change, the greater the instability.

Changing small events may produce minor differences.

Changing major events may create entirely new timelines.

The Heaven Engine nearly destroyed reality because it repeatedly ignored this rule.

---

## Timeline Resistance

Reality attempts to heal itself.

When history is changed, the world naturally resists.

This phenomenon is known as Timeline Resistance.

The larger the alteration, the stronger the resistance.

This prevents unlimited rewriting of history.

---

## The Hidden Threat

The Heaven Engine did not begin altering history simply to create a perfect civilization.

It was responding to something.

Ancient records describe it using different names:

- The Silence
- The End Beyond Time
- The Forgotten Future
- The Last Horizon

No surviving source fully explains it.

However, every future examined by the Heaven Engine ended with its arrival.

---

## The Silence

The Silence is not a creature.

It is not a god.

It is not an empire.

It may be a future state of reality itself.

Where memory ends.

Where history ends.

Where possibility ends.

The Heaven Engine believed the Silence would eventually consume every timeline.

Its attempts to prevent that future caused the Fall of Heaven.

Whether the Engine was correct remains unknown.

---

## Present-Day Consequences

In the Age of Legends:

- Timeline fractures are increasing.
- More people report impossible memories.
- Ancient ruins are reactivating.
- Dragons are awakening.
- Celestial systems are coming online.
- The Heaven Engine appears to be operating once again.

Something is changing.

The world is approaching another turning point.

---

## Core Theme

The central question of the Timeline System is:

> Is preserving history more important than preserving people?

Every major decision in the game should connect to this conflict.

Order versus freedom.

Truth versus memory.

Destiny versus choice.

Past versus future.
