# Factions Bible

## Purpose

The factions of Aetheria are not simply political groups.

Each faction represents a different answer to the central question of the world:

> What should be done with the legacy of Heaven?

Some seek restoration.

Some seek freedom.

Some seek truth.

Some seek power.

Some seek destruction.

---

# The Dragon Dynasty

## Overview

The Dragon Dynasty descends from the ancient dragon lineages that once served as guardians during the Age of Heaven.

Many dragons still remember fragments of the Celestial Age.

Their memories are incomplete.

Some remember Aetherion as a hero.

Others remember him as the ruler who doomed the world.

## Belief

Order must be preserved.

The mistakes of mortals created chaos.

Only strong guardians can protect civilization.

## Goal

Prevent another Fall.

Protect dangerous Celestial technology.

Control access to major ruins.

## View of Aetherion

Divided.

Some await his return.

Others fear it.

---

# The Naga Kingdom

## Overview

The Nagas are the keepers of memory.

During the final centuries of the Celestial Age, vast archives were entrusted to them.

They possess knowledge that no human kingdom remembers.

## Belief

History must be preserved.

Truth is more important than power.

## Goal

Protect the hidden archives.

Prevent knowledge from falling into the wrong hands.

Reveal the truth only when the world is ready.

## View of Aetherion

Complicated.

They know more about him than any other faction.

They refuse to reveal everything.

---

# The Celestial Remnants

## Overview

The Celestial Remnants are descendants, preserved minds, dormant systems, and surviving fragments of the old civilization.

Some exist as humans.

Some exist as artificial intelligences.

Some exist as something in between.

## Belief

The Fall was a mistake.

The Age of Heaven should return.

## Goal

Restore lost technology.

Reactivate ancient systems.

Rebuild what was destroyed.

## View of Aetherion

The greatest ruler in history.

Or the greatest failure.

The Remnants argue among themselves.

---

# The Spirit Clans

## Overview

The Spirit Clans are not a single organization.

They are countless spirit courts, guardian beings, ancestral entities, and ancient forces born from nature and memory.

Many emerged after the Fall of Heaven.

## Belief

Balance is sacred.

Neither Heaven nor mortals should dominate the world.

## Goal

Prevent reality from becoming unstable.

Contain Echo Zones.

Protect sacred locations.

## View of Aetherion

A mortal who reached too far.

---

# The Kingdoms of Men

## Overview

Most humans know little about the true history of the world.

They build kingdoms upon Celestial ruins without understanding what lies beneath them.

Many wars are fought over relics and sacred sites.

## Belief

Survival comes first.

Power determines destiny.

## Goal

Expand territory.

Acquire relics.

Gain influence.

## View of Aetherion

Myth.

Most do not even know he existed.

---

# The Keepers of the Veil

## Overview

A secret organization operating across multiple kingdoms.

Their members include scholars, spies, monks, explorers, and former Celestial researchers.

They investigate timeline fractures.

## Belief

Some knowledge is too dangerous for the world.

## Goal

Monitor Echo Zones.

Track timeline anomalies.

Prevent catastrophic discoveries.

## View of Aetherion

The key to understanding the Fall.

---

# The Void Cult

## Overview

The most feared faction in the world.

The Void Cult believes reality itself is corrupted.

They claim the current timeline is a false world created by endless historical manipulation.

## Belief

The broken world must end.

## Goal

Destroy the systems maintaining reality.

Accelerate the collapse of fractured timelines.

Allow history to reset.

## View of Aetherion

A sinner whose actions trapped existence inside an endless cycle.

---

# Relationship Matrix

Dragon Dynasty vs Void Cult

Order versus collapse.

Naga Kingdom vs Celestial Remnants

Preserve knowledge versus restore the past.

Spirit Clans vs Kingdoms of Men

Balance versus expansion.

Keepers of the Veil vs Everyone

Truth managed through secrecy.

---

# The Central Conflict

No faction fully agrees on what should happen.

The player's return as the latest incarnation of Aetherion threatens every existing balance.

Some factions will seek alliance.

Some will seek control.

Some will seek guidance.

Some will seek assassination.

As the truth of Heaven returns, every faction will be forced to choose:

Restore the past.

Protect the present.

Or create something entirely new.
